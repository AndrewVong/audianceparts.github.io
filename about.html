    • Our Company

  • Started out as SALBU Enterprise in 1986, the reputation for being one of the industry’s trusted and acknowledged retailers and suppliers of Automotive Aftermarket Parts was established. The demand expanded providing an opportunity to open several sister companies within Laguna and Cavite locations. In 2020, AUDIANCE PARTS OPC (Formerly Salbu Silang Auto Supply) is established as One Person Corporation.
  • Audiance Parts OPC former (Salbu Silang Auto Supply) has been recognized as one of the leading suppliers and retailers of Automotive Aftermarket and Industrial Parts mainly focused at CALABARZON areas. The Company provides individual consumers with an extensive selection of quality and best value products and services.
  • Today, the variety of products and services are continuously growing aiming to become one of the leading suppliers nationwide.
